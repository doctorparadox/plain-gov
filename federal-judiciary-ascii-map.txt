U.S. FEDERAL JUDICIARY — STRUCTURE & COMPOSITION (as of July 3, 2026)
│   Article III courts (appeals flow upward ▲) + Article I/IV courts alongside
│   Named only at the Supreme Court; lower-court chief judges rotate by seniority
│
■ SUPREME COURT OF THE UNITED STATES (SCOTUS)   —   9 justices · final appellate + limited original jurisdiction
│   │   Chief Justice presides; each justice is the "circuit justice" for one or more circuits

├─◆ U.S. COURTS OF APPEALS  —  13 circuits (intermediate appellate)   ▲ hear appeals from the district courts
│   │   12 regional circuits + 1 nationwide subject-matter circuit; each led by a Chief Judge (rotates by seniority)
│   │
│   ├─ D.C. Circuit ......... District of Columbia        [agency & administrative-law review; the "second-highest court"]
│   ├─ First Circuit ........ ME · MA · NH · RI · Puerto Rico
│   ├─ Second Circuit ....... CT · NY · VT
│   ├─ Third Circuit ........ DE · NJ · PA · U.S. Virgin Islands
│   ├─ Fourth Circuit ....... MD · VA · WV · NC · SC
│   ├─ Fifth Circuit ........ LA · MS · TX
│   ├─ Sixth Circuit ........ KY · MI · OH · TN
│   ├─ Seventh Circuit ...... IL · IN · WI
│   ├─ Eighth Circuit ....... AR · IA · MN · MO · NE · ND · SD
│   ├─ Ninth Circuit ........ AK · AZ · CA · HI · ID · MT · NV · OR · WA · Guam · N. Mariana Is.
│   ├─ Tenth Circuit ........ CO · KS · NM · OK · UT · WY
│   ├─ Eleventh Circuit ..... AL · FL · GA
│   └─ Federal Circuit ...... nationwide by subject-matter: patents · int'l trade · federal claims · veterans
│
├─◆ U.S. DISTRICT COURTS  —  94 districts (federal trial courts of general jurisdiction)   ▲ appeal to the regional circuit
│   │   ≥1 district per state (larger states split into 2–4) + D.C. + Puerto Rico + territorial courts
│   │
│   ├─ Units within each district:
│   │     ├─ U.S. Bankruptcy Court ....... one unit per district (Article I, under the district court)
│   │     └─ U.S. Magistrate Judges ...... assist district judges (pretrial matters & misdemeanors)
│   └─ Territorial district courts (Article IV): Guam · N. Mariana Islands · U.S. Virgin Islands
│
├─◆ SPECIALIZED ARTICLE III COURT
│   └─ U.S. Court of International Trade ......... customs & international-trade cases   ▲ appeals to the Federal Circuit
│
├─◆ SURVEILLANCE COURTS  (staffed by designated Article III judges)
│   ├─ Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Court (FISC) ...... reviews/approves FISA surveillance applications
│   └─ Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Court of Review ... reviews FISC denials   ▲ then SCOTUS
│
├─◆ ARTICLE I / LEGISLATIVE COURTS  (created by Congress; outside the Article III judiciary)
│   ├─ U.S. Tax Court ............................ ▲ appeals to the taxpayer's regional circuit
│   ├─ U.S. Court of Federal Claims .............. monetary claims against the U.S.   ▲ appeals to the Federal Circuit
│   ├─ U.S. Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims . ▲ appeals to the Federal Circuit
│   └─ U.S. Court of Appeals for the Armed Forces  military justice   ▲ review by SCOTUS
│
└─◆ JUDICIAL ADMINISTRATION  (governance & support, not adjudication)
    ├─ Judicial Conference of the United States ... national policy-making body (Chief Justice presides)
    ├─ Administrative Office of the U.S. Courts .... day-to-day administration of the branch
    ├─ Federal Judicial Center .................... research & judicial education
    └─ U.S. Sentencing Commission ................. sets federal sentencing guidelines
